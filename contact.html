About this site

Decoding Autism Now

Decoding Autism Now publishes the Biology of Autism (BoA) / Immune-Derived Autism (IDA) framework — a systems-biology cascade model connecting gut pH dysregulation, immune activation, IDO1/kynurenine pathway dysregulation, SST-14 interneuron suppression, and neuropeptide cascade failure to the observable ASD phenotype.

All framework documentation is available at no cost at decodingautismnow.com.

For families

Where to start

If you are new to the framework, the Parent's Guide and FAQ pages are the best starting points. The L1-79 analysis includes a downloadable plain-language PDF written for families to share with their child's physician.

For clinicians & researchers

Framework documentation

The full cascade model is documented across the Biology of Autism suite. Peer-reviewed anchor publication: Fourie & Armstrong, Medical Research Archives 12(10), 2024. doi:10.18103/mra.v12i10.5984

Important

Not clinical guidance

Decoding Autism Now does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations for individual cases. All clinical decisions require qualified physician oversight. The IDA framework is a research-informed theoretical model.

i

Theoretical framework — not clinical guidance. The Biology of Autism / IDA cascade is a systems-biology model integrating peer-reviewed findings across immunology, metabolism, gut biology, and neuroscience into a proposed mechanistic map. Individual components are supported by published research; the full integrated cascade has not been validated as a unified model in large clinical trials. It is intended as a research-informed framework — not a diagnostic tool or treatment protocol. All intervention decisions require qualified clinical oversight.
